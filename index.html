How to use: Measure the bellows draw from the film plane to the rear of the lens. Enter that value along with the focal length of your lens.

Formula: Bellows factor = (extension ÷ focal length)². Stops = log₂(factor).

Rule of thumb: At 1:1 magnification (extension = 2× focal length) add 2 stops. At 1:2 magnification add 1 stop.
